Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to a Premium account?
Upgrading is simple! Just click on the "Upgrade Now" button and follow the payment instructions. Once your payment is processed, your account will be instantly upgraded to Premium status with all features unlocked.
Can I switch between account types?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your account at any time. If you downgrade from Premium to Free, you'll maintain access to Premium features until the end of your current billing cycle. Any listings beyond the Free account limit will be automatically deactivated.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, digital wallets, and cryptocurrency payments including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and our native STELLAR tokens. Enterprise customers can also arrange for direct bank transfers or invoicing.
Is there a discount for annual subscriptions?
Yes! When you choose annual billing, you'll receive a 20% discount compared to monthly payments. This means you'll pay for 10 months and get 2 months free, saving \u20ae 718 per year on the Premium plan.
What happens to my listings if I cancel Premium?
If you downgrade to a Free account, you'll be limited to 3 active listings. Any additional listings will be automatically deactivated but not deleted. You can choose which 3 listings to keep active, and you can reactivate others if you upgrade again or remove some active listings.
Can I try Premium features before committing?
Absolutely! New users can try Premium features free for 14 days. You'll need to provide payment information, but you won't be charged until the trial period ends. You can cancel anytime during the trial with no obligation.
